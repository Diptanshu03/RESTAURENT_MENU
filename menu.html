Coder Cafe's Menu

STRATER

  • Chicken Tikka Kabab

    This kabab features marinated chicken pieces (often boneless) skewered and grilled to perfection. The marinade includes yogurt, spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric, along with lemon juice for tanginess.

    60₹ - 4 Pieces

  • Galouti Kabab

    Originating from Lucknow, these kababs are known for their melt-in-your-mouth texture. They are made from finely minced meat (usually lamb or beef) mixed with aromatic spices, raw papaya (to tenderize the meat), and then pan-fried or grilled.

    80₹ - 4 Pieces

  • Seekh Kabab

    These are typically made from minced meat (usually lamb or beef) mixed with spices like cumin, coriander, and chili powder. The meat is then molded onto skewers and grilled or roasted.

    90₹/Plate

  • Hara Bhara Kabab

    A vegetarian option, these kababs are made from a mixture of mashed potatoes, spinach, peas, and various spices. They are shaped into patties and either shallow-fried or baked until crisp on the outside.

    50₹ - 4 Pieces
